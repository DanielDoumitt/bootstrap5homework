A website about a game I love!

I love to play videogames, and one of my favorite games to play is Terraria. Terraria is a closed world survival game where a player gathers loot, gear, builds, and fights bosses/entities.

Terria town NPC's

Terraria has a multitude of Npc's in the game to talk to and trade with a couple of them are very useful but some are not very helpful.

Important NPC's

  • Dryad
    • The Dryad sells planter boxes for you to plant seeds. Seeds are used to make potions.
  • Nurse
    • The Nurse will heal you if you pay her which can be helpful if you need a quick heal.
  • Goblin Tinkerer
    • The Goblin Tinkerer lets you reforge your items which can make them more powerful but you must pay him for each reforge.
  • Guide
    • The Guide tells you helpful tips when you talk to him. He also will show you crafting recipies for free.

Terraria game stages

There are three main Terraria game stages, they are early game, prehardmode, hardmode. Early game is building your house and gathering materials for the first bosses. Prehardmode is the frist couple of bosses you fight up until the sixth boss. Hardmode is achived after the sixth boss is killed and harder mobs start to come and you kill the remaining bosses in the game.

The slime mount allows the player toi ride a slime that damages mobs by bouncing on them.
The pogo stick mount is not very helpful but is very cool not much that it does, it is just a pogo stick.
The ufo mount is a rideable ufo that allows the player to fly as long as they want.
